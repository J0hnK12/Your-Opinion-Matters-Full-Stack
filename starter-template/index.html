Your Opinion Matters

Your Opinion Matters is a Would You Rather Game that has a special little twist. The twist that this contains is that you have 10 questions but instead of 2 choices, you have 4 choices. This game is fun because you get to see how many other people agree with your decision and fun for the whole family to play.

group

User Experience

After each question, there is a button that allows you to submit you answer, after clicking it, you will see what percentage chose the same choice as you as well as people who chose differently. You can also play this game with your friends and get their input for the same questions and maybe you can get to know them better.

settings

Easy to work with

This game is easy to navigate as well as the selection of your answers. The questions are in dropdown menus that are easy to select from.
